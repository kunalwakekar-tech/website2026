Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the process take?

While every business is unique, the average timeframe from engagement to closing is typically 6 to 9 months. Our goal is to move as quickly as the market allows without sacrificing value.

Will my employees or competitors find out?

Confidentiality is the cornerstone of our firm. We do not use public listing sites. Potential buyers are only given high-level, non-identifying data until a strict Non-Disclosure Agreement is executed and their identity is verified.

What is my business worth?

Valuation is determined by multiple factors including industry trends, historical growth, and the strength of your management team. We offer a complimentary preliminary analysis to provide you with a baseline range.
